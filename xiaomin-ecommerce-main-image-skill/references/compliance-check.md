# 主图质量与合规检查

Use this file before final delivery and when critiquing a main image.

## Thumbnail Readability

- Product is recognizable within 1 second.
- Product occupies enough area for search listing view.
- Main headline is readable at small size.
- Badges are not too small, too many, or decorative-only.
- The image reads as a sellable product main image, not as a banner, poster, presentation slide, or design review board.
- For activity/coupon main images, the offer must also be readable within 1 second.

## Product Fidelity

- Packaging shape and perspective remain plausible.
- Logo/brand feel is not distorted beyond recognition.
- SKU facts are not changed: flavor, color, size, count, version, stage, model.
- Product is not hidden behind props, animals, hands, food texture, or text.
- Product edges are clean. There must be no visible screenshot rectangle, leftover web-page background, mismatched cutout edge, or low-resolution source artifact in a final image.
- If the source image is only a screenshot or marketplace preview, classify the output as a concept/direction unless the product has been cleanly rebuilt or edited with credible commercial quality.

## Visual Hierarchy

- Product is the largest or most important subject.
- Background supports product, not competing with it.
- Decorative arcs, icons, labels, and props do not create clutter.
- There is one clear focal path: product -> headline -> badges.
- Headline and badges must not form a giant top panel that competes with the product unless the platform and campaign explicitly require that.
- Do not use an obvious hard horizontal split unless it is an intentional high-end layout and product still dominates.
- For promotional main images, check the three zones separately: event/campaign zone, hero product/benefit zone, conversion offer zone.
- A large price/coupon bar is acceptable only when real supplied offer data is the main click reason.

## Commercial Finish

Reject the image as not marketplace-ready if any of these are true:

- It is just the supplied image placed on a new background.
- It looks like a quick collage, PPT slide, or internal review mockup.
- It contains process copy such as `主图优化`, `设计稿`, `方案`, `视觉优化`, `示意`.
- The product has a different lighting direction from props or background.
- Decorative elements look like flat stickers, clip art, or synthetic filler.
- Food texture looks fake, plastic, blurry, or unrelated to the package.
- Promotional blocks look unrelated to the product or hide the SKU.
- The design has more than four competing major text groups without a clear reading order.

## Copy Risk

Check for unsupported or risky wording:

- Medical or disease claims.
- Absolute claims: `第一`, `最好`, `最强`, `100%`, `零风险`.
- Unprovided certifications, awards, official endorsements.
- Unprovided price, promotion, coupon, date, shipping promise.
- Unprovided gifts, buy-X-get-Y mechanics, membership packages, threshold discounts, or activity names.
- Nutrition/ingredient claims not visible or supplied.
- Any price, mechanism, benefit, gift, activity time, or required display text that was not included in the confirmed commercial brief.

For pet food, avoid unsupported claims about:
- 治疗
- 免疫
- 发育促进
- 补钙
- 肠胃改善
- 医疗处方

Use safer factual wording:
- `湿粮质地`
- `超柔慕斯`
- `幼犬阶段`
- `罐装规格`
- `细腻肉泥感`

## Platform Fit

- Tmall/JD: brand-clean, restrained, high trust.
- Taobao/Douyin: more scene and click appeal, still product-dominant.
- Amazon: white background for strict main image.
- Xiaohongshu: lifestyle/editorial, low hard-sell.
- Tmall/Taobao campaign: event strip + hero + offer bar is acceptable when activity data is provided.
- JD campaign: cleaner benefit/spec cards, less decorative clutter.

## AI Image Specific Checks

- Text is not garbled.
- Product does not gain random extra logos or labels.
- Pet/human anatomy looks natural if included.
- Food texture is appetizing and plausible.
- No watermark or QR code.
- No accidental competitor brand.
- Product packaging, food props, shadows, reflections, and typography belong to the same visual world.
- Offer numerals are correct and not hallucinated.
- Gift modules contain only supplied gifts and do not create fake products.
- The generated image matches the confirmed confirmation card. If it adds unconfirmed price/offer/benefit text, reject and regenerate.

## Fix Priority

If an image is weak, fix in this order:

1. Product size and clarity.
2. Packaging/logo fidelity.
3. Main headline readability.
4. Scene/background simplification.
5. Badge count and copy accuracy.
6. Color/lighting polish.

If the image is structurally wrong, fix in this order:

1. Rebuild product cutout/composition instead of pasting the screenshot.
2. Remove internal/process copy.
3. Replace fake decorative props with realistic texture or remove them.
4. Reduce poster-like text blocks.
5. Re-check platform fit and thumbnail readability.

If an activity image is structurally weak, fix in this order:

1. Clarify the three-zone hierarchy.
2. Reduce competing text groups.
3. Make product or texture more recognizable.
4. Move price/coupon/gift into a single conversion module.
5. Remove unsupplied activity claims.
