# 平台风格适配

Use this file when the user names a platform or channel.

## 天猫 / Tmall

- Brand-first, premium, clean, not noisy.
- Product polish and packaging fidelity matter.
- Copy should be restrained: headline + 1-3 badges.
- Avoid discount-heavy low-end visuals unless for official campaign.
- For Tmall shopping festivals, use official-feeling top event strips, rounded white content cards, and bottom coupon/price bars. Keep brand trust even when offer copy is large.

## 京东 / JD

- Clean, rational, trust-oriented.
- White, blue, red, and light gray systems work well.
- Specs and product recognition matter.
- Good for appliances, electronics, pet food, health-adjacent categories.
- Use clear price/spec cards and less decorative atmosphere than Taobao. Benefits and specs should be easy to scan.

## 淘宝

- More flexible and click-driven.
- Scene, texture, benefit callouts, and lively composition are acceptable.
- Still keep product as the dominant subject.
- Price, coupon, gift, and activity modules can be visually louder. Use strong bottom bars, diagonals, labels, and contrast, but avoid chaotic text collisions.

## 抖音电商

- More immediate, high-contrast, content-native.
- Use stronger scenario and visible result/texture.
- Text should be short and punchy.
- Avoid making it look like a long video cover unless requested.
- The first message should be understood in under 1 second. Favor one big benefit or one big price mechanic, not a full offer sheet.

## 拼多多

- Direct value communication, larger copy, stronger contrast.
- Still avoid chaotic low-quality clutter unless the user explicitly wants low-price promotion.
- Price/promotion copy must be supplied by the user.
- Value hierarchy may put price first, then product, then gift/quantity. Product still must be identifiable.

## 小红书

- Lifestyle, editorial, clean, natural lighting.
- Product in realistic use scene.
- Softer typography and more whitespace.
- Avoid hard-sell promotional blocks.
- If campaign information is needed, use a clean card/module rather than a loud bottom price strip.

## Amazon

- Main image usually needs white background and product-only style.
- Avoid extra props, badges, or heavy text for strict primary image.
- Secondary images may use lifestyle, infographics, and comparison.

## Shopee / Lazada

- Strong visual clarity at small thumbnail size.
- More color and badges are acceptable.
- Keep product recognizable; avoid overcrowding.

## No Platform Provided

Use balanced brand ecommerce:
- product-dominant
- clean background
- restrained copy
- professional commercial photography
- no extreme discount styling

If the user provides examples that are clearly platform campaign creatives, infer a Taobao/Tmall campaign style rather than applying this quiet default.
