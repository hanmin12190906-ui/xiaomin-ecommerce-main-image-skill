# 主图表现模板选择

Use this file after `main-image-types.md` when deciding how the main image should look.

## Key Distinction

Main image type answers: **what commercial job does the image do?**

Visual template answers: **what layout style should execute that job?**

Examples:

- `selling-point + product-texture`: product pack plus appetizing texture.
- `selling-point + pet-scene + brand-trust`: product pack plus pet scene and brand-safe headline.
- `gift/member + service-mechanism`: product plus member coupon or delivery module.
- `campaign + campaign-conversion`: event strip, product hero, offer module.

## Template Archetypes

### 1. Product Texture Template

Use for snacks, pet treats, wet food, beauty texture, beverages, and material-led products.

Layout:
- Product pack is large and central.
- Realistic texture/use-result appears as secondary foreground.
- One headline plus 1-2 small badges.

Best for:
- Freeze-dried treats
- Canned food / mousse
- Chocolate / chips / drinks
- Beauty texture

Avoid:
- Fake crumbs, clip-art food pieces, random ingredient explosions.

### 2. Pet Scene Template

Use for pet food, pet snacks, pet care, cat/dog daily-use products, and products where pet presence increases trust.

Layout:
- Product pack remains the largest or equal-largest subject.
- Pet appears as secondary lifestyle element, never covering product or key text.
- Food bowl, treat pieces, clean home/studio background can support the product.
- Headline sits on the left/top with generous white space.

Best for:
- Cat/dog food
- Pet treats
- Pet nutrition products
- Pet cleaning/care products

Copy:
- Use category and stage/benefit only when confirmed.
- Keep pet health claims safe; avoid treatment/immunity/digestion medical claims unless legally supplied.

Avoid:
- Cute pet dominating the image while product becomes small.
- Random pet breed changes if breed matters.
- Overly childish paw-print decoration for premium brands.

### 3. Brand Trust Template

Use for high-trust brands, imported goods, professional pet brands, official-store style product pages, and premium daily goods.

Layout:
- Brand logo or brand area is clear but not fake or re-created as an unsupported official badge.
- Product pack is large and clean.
- Background is restrained: white, cream, brand color blocks, or soft gradients.
- Typography is clean, bold, and stable.

Best for:
- Royal Canin, Orijen, Hill's, Dove, usmile, premium FMCG.

Avoid:
- Inventing `官方`, `旗舰店`, awards, certifications, or platform badges unless supplied.

### 4. Service Mechanism Template

Use only when the user supplies service, coupon, membership, delivery, or after-sales mechanics.

Layout:
- Bottom or side service module separated from product hero.
- Product remains recognizable.
- Service module uses simple icon + exact confirmed copy.

Common modules:
- `入会建档享5元券`
- `顺丰发货 | 送货上门`
- `冰袋冷运`
- `买2赠1`

Avoid:
- Adding service promises not supplied by the user.
- Making service module bigger than product unless conversion is the goal.

### 5. Campaign Conversion Template

Use for price, coupon, event, membership, gifts, and shopping-festival campaigns.

Layout:
- Three-zone hierarchy: event/campaign zone, product/benefit hero, conversion offer module.
- Large numbers only when price/discount is confirmed.

Avoid:
- Over-cleaning campaign images so the offer disappears.
- Inventing any price, date, coupon, gift, or event name.

### 6. White Shelf Template

Use for platform search/catalog clarity.

Layout:
- Product cutout on white or very light background.
- Text minimal or absent.
- Product occupies 70-88% of canvas.

Avoid:
- Lifestyle clutter.
- Decorative badge overload.

### 7. Lifestyle Editorial Template

Use for Xiaohongshu, premium gifts, home scenes, beauty/lifestyle products.

Layout:
- Product integrated into a refined use scene.
- Text is restrained.
- Mood and aspiration matter, but product still must be readable.

Avoid:
- Turning the image into a poster where product is too small.

## Pet Product Defaults

For pet products, offer or choose among:

1. `product-texture`: safest for SKU fidelity and food texture.
2. `pet-scene + brand-trust`: best for trust, warmth, and click-through.
3. `service-mechanism`: only if service/coupon/delivery is confirmed.

If the user provides a pet-scene example, prefer `pet-scene + brand-trust`. Add `service-mechanism` only for confirmed service copy.

## Prompt Requirements

When generating, include a `Visual template:` line and state:

- Which template(s) are selected.
- Which visual elements are allowed.
- Which modules must not appear because they were not confirmed.
- Product size priority.
- Relationship between product, pet/person, texture, badges, and service modules.
